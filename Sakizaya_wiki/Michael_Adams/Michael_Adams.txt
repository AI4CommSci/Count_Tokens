Michael Adams (麥可•亞當斯)
tatama, nalecuhan i 19 bulad 1 a demiad, 1963 a mihcaan, Michael Adams, miunduay. 
Michael Adams unu syubay kanas-mali miunduay nu Amilika NBA nisakaput, u misimaw zikuzay mimaliay.
Michael Adams i 1985 a mihcan NBA sapapili’ mimaliay a kawaw, saka 3 ku uwac atu silsil 66, namapili’ tu nu Sacramento Kings putiput.
