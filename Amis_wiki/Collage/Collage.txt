O Collage ko tatolsa a romadiway i Taywan. O romadiway i Collage ci Natsuko Lariyod aci Ông Ka-koân. O somowalay to Pangcah(Amis), Holo, Nihongo, ato Amerika ko Collage i raradiw.... 
"Collagist" han to haliCollage'ay a tamdaw.

O Histori (Kongko)
I 2018 a miheca, mitayal to "Seikai Sakacecay - O Mireformay a Contest" ko Band no 'Adingo(閃靈) ato The Bell.
I 2019 a miheca, ira ko "Collage" a misatapang i StreetVoice ato i YouTube a miteli to raradiw no niyam.
I 2019 a miheca, 3 a folad, 27 a romi'ad, mitayal to "N1 Paty - O Raradiw no Tamdaw no Nanguaq" ko Nanguaq, Payleday Records... ato Collage.
