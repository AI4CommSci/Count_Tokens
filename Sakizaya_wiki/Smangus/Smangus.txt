 Smangus（司馬庫斯部落） 
i Hsinchu a kuwan nu Kalinku ku niyazu’ nu Smangus. u kasalumaluma’ nu Smangus sa, 38 ku luma’. kumud nu kasabinawlan sa, 176 ku tademawan. kasabinawlan nu Ingcumin sa, 173 ku tademaw, pakalatu 98%.
u zuma sa, cay ku Ingcumin, 3 ku tademaw, pakalatu 2%.
u kasabinacadan, Tayal 85%, Amis 1%, Paiwan 2%, Bunun 1%, zumazuma 10%.
