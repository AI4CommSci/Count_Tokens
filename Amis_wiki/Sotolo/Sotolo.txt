Sotolo o koli（kuwaping a sowal：首陀羅；Fan a sowal：शूद्र；Romaan tilid：Śūdra）: o sakasepat kasasa’er ko ’orip ilaloma’ no kasasa’er-no’orip a faco no Indo, o maomahay, ikakaay a misatofangay ato misamoraday konini.itiya ho o matayalay to ma’acekan tayal tora mipacokay to ‘a’adopen ato mipatayay to tamdaw a tayal ato mifalahay to tai’ ko tayal, orasaka, cowako tatefingen koninian tamadaw (不可接觸者; untouchables) hananay.
