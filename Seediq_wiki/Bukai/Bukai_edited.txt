= Bukai =
Gaga mniq Nantowseyn Jnaysyang ka alang Bukai.
250 sapah ka kneegu na sapah, 1,057 hiyi ka kana knhbragan na hiyi. Tnpusu seejiq o mangal 96% niqan 1,012 hiyi sp, rahuq na uxay tnpusu seejiq o mangal 4% niqan  45 hiyi koia. 
Kana ka kleegan seejiq ga, Bunun 91%, Tayal 2%, Amis 1%, Seejiq/Seediq/Seejiq/Seejiq/Sediq 1%.

Pusu nniqan alang Mniq nyusan Nato o Tg2 egu ka seejiq alang Bukai tg1 paru o alang Rwona .

Nniqan alang Nyusan Nanto nsgan Jnay alnag Fac .

Gasuc nniqan Alang hraan hidaw hiya o sslian qsiya Bukai, gqiyan hidaw hiya o kssiyaw keelug qhuni Bukai, nniqan yayung mdudul kiya, niqan ciway saph pyasan ka ruwan alang hiya.

Lnglingay tiri Pusu mniq ssiyaw yayaung ka dxgal alang nii, kana knjisan dgiyaq nii o 800mm.
