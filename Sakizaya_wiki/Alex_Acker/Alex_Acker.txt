亞利克斯·阿克，為美國NBA聯盟的職業籃球運動員。他在2005年的NBA選秀中第2輪第30順位被底特律活塞選中。
Alex Maurice Acker is an American professional basketball player who last played for Pallacanestro Cantù of the Lega Basket Serie A (LBA). He played college basketball for Pepperdine University.
