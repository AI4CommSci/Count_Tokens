Dave Batton nalecuhan i Baltimore, Maryland, nanu syubay kanas-mali miunduay nu Amilika NBA nisakaput, pahanhanan tu kawaw ayza. Dave Batton i 1978 a mihcan NBA sapapili' mimaliay a kawaw, saka 3 ku uwac atu silsil 62, namapili' tu nu New Jersey Nets putiput.
3 bulad 26 demiad, 1956 a mihca, Dave Batton, miunduay.
