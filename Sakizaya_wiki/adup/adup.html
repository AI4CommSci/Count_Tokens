u kamu nu adup[mikawaway-kalumyiti | mikawaway tu kalumyiti sakatizeng bangu]

adup 狩獵, u kamu nu Amilika: hunting

aadupen 動物

miadup 打獵

miadupay 獵人

piadupan 獵場

u zumazuma nu tinaku a kamu[mikawaway-kalumyiti | mikawaway tu kalumyiti sakatizeng bangu]

adup[mikawaway-kalumyiti | mikawaway tu kalumyiti sakatizeng bangu]

adup sa ku selal sa u makudusay a sizi.

makudusay: u uzip nu tademaw atu aadupen, caykayadah ku titi, azihan tanuukak saan.

aadupen[mikawaway-kalumyiti | mikawaway tu kalumyiti sakatizeng bangu]

i buyubuyu'an ku aadupen a mauzip.

i bayubayuan ku buting a mauzip.

miadup[mikawaway-kalumyiti | mikawaway tu kalumyiti sakatizeng bangu]

miadup kita u kapah ayza.

lalimiadup ku kapah, tayza bayu' miadup atu mideteng.

miadupay[mikawaway-kalumyiti | mikawaway tu kalumyiti sakatizeng bangu]

u miadupay ku acawa nu maku.

u miadupay ku wawa nu maku, matineng miadup ku wawa atu acawa nu maku.

piadupan[mikawaway-kalumyiti | mikawaway tu kalumyiti sakatizeng bangu]

u piadupan aku sa i nuzikuzan nina buyu'.

nu makuay a piadupan itiza i nuzikuzan nina buyu'.

namakayniay a nasulitan nasakamuan atu natinenganː[mikawaway-kalumyiti | mikawaway tu kalumyiti sakatizeng bangu]

malaalitin tu i hekalay atu zumaay a natinenganː[mikawaway-kalumyiti | mikawaway tu kalumyiti sakatizeng bangu]
