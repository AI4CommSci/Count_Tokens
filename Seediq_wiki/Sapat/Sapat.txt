= Sapat（掃叭頂部落） =
Gaga mniq Hwareyseyn Jweysweysyang ka alang Sapat.

Kana ka kleegan seejiq ga, Amis 20%, Bunun 7%, Tayal 1%, Puyuma 1%, ni rahuq na o 1%.
