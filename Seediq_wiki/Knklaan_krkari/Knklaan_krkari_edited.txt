 Kari Tgdaya Kesun daha knklaan krkari we, knklaan pskadi pusu bale so hengak na kari, ooda tmkari, gaya na kari ma dungus na kari. 
Niqan duma knklaan pnswayan na knklaan krkari, so ka knklaan hengak (Phonetics ), knklaan gaya hengak (Phonology ), knklaan tmkari biciq (Morphology ), knklaan gaya nkari (Syntax ), knklaan dungus nkari (Semantics ), ma knklaan skari (Pragmatics ).
