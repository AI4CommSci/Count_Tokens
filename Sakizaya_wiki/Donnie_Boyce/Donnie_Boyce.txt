Donnie Boyce nalecuhan i Chicago, Illinois, nanu syubay kanas-mali miunduay nu Amilika NBA nisakaput, pahanhanan tu kawaw ayza.
Donnie Boyce i 1995 a mihcan NBA sapapili’ mimaliay a kawaw, saka 2 ku uwac atu silsil 42, namapili’ tu nu Atlanta Hawks putiput.
