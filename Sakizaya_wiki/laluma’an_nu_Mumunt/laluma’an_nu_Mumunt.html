makayzaay i Wikipitiya

dadiw nu sulda atu lamal(Amilika: A Song of Ice and Fire), u nisangaan nu misulitay ci George Raymond Richard Martin,u kungku nu nilalamelan nu laylay atu malawlaay nadatengan. izaw ku pituay a pu(部) nu ulic nu nida.

malagiki nu HBO i 2011 a mihcaan.


laluma'an nu Mumunt(Amilika a kamu: House of Mormont, kamu nu Hulam:莫爾蒙家族) i tumayay a subal(Amilika a kamu: Bear Island, kamu nu Hulam:熊島 ), u cacayay satenesay a laluma'an i nuamisan.

u bayabay nu laluma'an nu Mumunt sa, u cacayay a tumay, mutideng i labu nu lanlangdaway a cebecebedan; kamu nu laluma'an nu Mumunt sa, u "i tini kita mutideng." (Amilika a kamu: Here We Stand, kamu nu Hulam:昂首屹立)

laylay nu laluma'an nu Mumunt[mikawaway-kalumyiti | mikawaway tu kalumyiti sakatizeng bangu]

u kungku kasumamadan sananay, u mukeliday a ung (王) ci Lacik Stak mademec cinida i Tumayay a subal a tademaw sa, patubeli han ci Lacik Stak ku Tumayay a subal tu laluma'an nu Mumunt. kyu, malamukeliday a laluma'an nu Munmunt hananay, sisa mitengil ku heni tu kamu nu laluma'an nu Stak.

u kasahenulay a tademaw nu laluma'an nu Mumunt[mikawaway-kalumyiti | mikawaway tu kalumyiti sakatizeng bangu]

Ciu Mumunt[mikawaway-kalumyiti | mikawaway tu kalumyiti sakatizeng bangu]

saayaway sa, u mukeliday nu laluma'an nu Mumunt, kyu patelacay a wayway nu wawa nida ci Cyola sa, malahitay nu misebangay tu labii hananay, u mukeliday nu hitay nu misebengay tu labii hananay.

Cyola Mumunt[mikawaway-kalumyiti | mikawaway tu kalumyiti sakatizeng bangu]


Linis Hatel[mikawaway-kalumyiti | mikawaway tu kalumyiti sakatizeng bangu]

u acawa ni Cola Mumunt, i nu dikudan nu patalacay ni Cola sa, miliyas ci nida i Cyolaan sa. ayda sa, u acawa nu packayay nu Lis.

Meyki Mumunt[mikawaway-kalumyiti | mikawaway tu kalumyiti sakatizeng bangu]

nu dikudan a mukeliday nu Tumayay a subal, sibukes tu abuabuwan, apuyu' ku udip nida, nika, u caay kau mamasapen (很厲害) a hitay.

Liana Mumunt[mikawaway-kalumyiti | mikawaway tu kalumyiti sakatizeng bangu]

sipangangan i ci Liana Stakan hananay. u sasabaay a wawana ni Meyki Mumunt, sakamu sa ku katuuday, u sadikuday a tumay ci Liana Mumunt. u sadikuday a mukeliday nu Tumayay a subal hananay.

napatigami ci Stanis Paylatiyung tu ci Liana Mumuntan, pitengili cinidaan sa, nika "caaypitengil tu zumaay a mukeliday, uyda dada' ci Stak ku tatengilan." sa ci Liana Mumunt. u sibutulay a tatayna.

malalitin tu ihekaay atu zumaay a natinengan[mikawaway-kalumyiti | mikawaway tu kalumyiti sakatizeng bangu]

nasulitan ni Padakaw Lami' 2020.
