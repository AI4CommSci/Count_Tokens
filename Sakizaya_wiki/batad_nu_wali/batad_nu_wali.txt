batad nu wali (kamu nu Hulam: 遠東[1])
u katinengan nu salengacay a tademaw (白種人) i "tipay" (西方).
u batad nu wali hananay ku sawalian nu “nutipan nu Europe” (西歐).  
i tepal ku “untipan nu Europe” (西歐), u “tepalay a wali” (近東) hananay. izaw ku “nuwalian nu O-Cuo” (East Europe, 東歐), “nuwalitimulan nu  Europe” (東南歐), “nuamisan nu Africa (北非)” atu Turkey (土耳其). u “inutebanan nu walian” (中東) hananay izaw ku “nuwalian nu Africa” (東非), Li-Fan-Te (黎凡特, Lavent[2]), A-La-Po pangkiw a subal (阿拉伯半島) atu “inutebanan nu Asia”(中亞).   

“batad nu wali” saan sa, namaka kakitidaan atu kitakit nu European a tademaw (歐洲人), u lala' i batad nu wali mueneng ku takuliyaway a tademaw (黃種人). sakawalian sabataday a China (中國), Korea North (朝鮮, 北韓), atu Japan (日本, ), u “nuwalian nu Asia” (東亞) hananay. sisa u batad nu wali saan sa. izaw ku henay Siberia (西伯利亞), u “nuamisan nu Asia” (北亞) hananay, atu “walitimulan nu Asia” (東南亞).
