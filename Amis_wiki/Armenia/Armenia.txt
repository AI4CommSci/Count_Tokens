Armenia（亞美尼亞）

I tini i 40 00 N, 45 00 E, i Asiya ko Armeniya, i kawali no Turki (土耳其).
Polong no sekalay i 29,743 km2, saka 143 ko katata’ang i hekal.
O sekalay no sera i, 28,203 km2, no nanom a sekalay i, 1,540 km2.
Polong i 3,051,250 ko tamdaw.

sera (土地)
Masakilac ko sera o malo kakaomahen(農業) a sera 59.70%, Malo no kilakilangan(林業) a sera 9.10%, malo no roma to a sera 31.20%.

siyoto (首都)

katomirengan no kitakit a romi’ad (國家紀念日)
