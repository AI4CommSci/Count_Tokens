Pungudan（大梅部落）

90% ko ka’aloman no Yincomin, polong han i, 323  ko tamdaw; o roma sato i, 10% ko ka’aloman no roma a finacadan, polong han i,  36 ko tamdaw.
O pa-sin-to(百分比) no ka’aloman no tamdaw no kasafinacadan i, ko Paiwan86%, Amis1%, Bunun1%, Rukai1%, roma1%.
