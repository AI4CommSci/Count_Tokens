 Lucius Allen (魯西斯·艾倫) 
9 bulad 26 demiad, 1947 a mihca, Lucius Allen, miunduay. 
Lucius Allen nalecuhan i Kansas City, Kansas, nanu syubay kanas-mali miunduay nu Amilika NBA nisakaput, pahanhanan tu kawaw ayza.
Lucius Allen i 1969 a mihcan NBA sapapili’ mimaliay a kawaw, saka 1 ku uwac atu silsil 3, namapili’ tu nu Seattle SuperSonics putiput.
