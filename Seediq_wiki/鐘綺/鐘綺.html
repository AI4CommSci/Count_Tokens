 Snluan qcinuh:藝人Zhong Qi (born April 8, 1994), also known by her birth name Tu Minqi, is a Taiwanese singer-songwriter and English magazine lecturer. She gained recognition in 2018 for her collaboration with Yang Ying on the theme song for movie - Taipei, I Love You, as well as her participation in the television talent show Jungle Voice.
