20171110-Taywan Yincumin Wikipayke mulecuhan patizneg a kawaw maleheciay pasingbung matatengil[mikawaway-kalumyiti | mikawaway tu kalumyiti sakatizeng bangu]

2017年11月10日-台灣原住民族維基百科孵育場建置計畫成果記者會

tatukian(時間)[mikawaway-kalumyiti | mikawaway tu kalumyiti sakatizeng bangu]

2017 a mihcan 11 a bulad 10 a demiad (sakalima a demiad nu lipay) 09:00-11:00

puenengan(地點)[mikawaway-kalumyiti | mikawaway tu kalumyiti sakatizeng bangu]

Taywan, Taypak, Secang luma' nalimaan salung (市長官邸藝文沙龍)

telungay(主持人)[mikawaway-kalumyiti | mikawaway tu kalumyiti sakatizeng bangu]

Akamycoco, Siunhaw (Zhengce tabakiay a cacudadan Yincumincu gankiw tebanay)

sakamuay (致詞者)[mikawaway-kalumyiti | mikawaway tu kalumyiti sakatizeng bangu]

Pasubana'ay a Pu Caicinghua kilulay 教育部蔡清華次長

Lipuying Cengtiencay lipuweiying 立法院鄭天財立法委員

Yingcumincu weiyuenhuy Yiwan Nawei kilulay 原民會伊萬那威副主委

Haka weiyuenhuy kilulay 客家委員會副主委

Cengce tabakiy a cacudadan LIM Siu-theh sasaydang 政治大學林修澈教授

tayni mitengilay(與會者)[mikawaway-kalumyiti | mikawaway tu kalumyiti sakatizeng bangu]

Wikipayke misulitay a saydang, Pasubana'ay a Pu, Yucumincu weiyuenhuy, Chengce tabakiaya cacudadan, Taypak secenfu, Haka weiyuenhuy, Lipuying

singbung kisiya

matatengil a lacul(程序內容)[mikawaway-kalumyiti | mikawaway tu kalumyiti sakatizeng bangu]

0930-0935 miteka, mipalaw ku Tayan tubtub 開場，泰雅族口簧琴演出

0935-0940 misiwkay tu Wikipayke patizeng palecuhan a kawaw 維基百科孵育場計畫簡介

0940-0950 iga: miazih ku iayaway a mikawaway-kalumyitiay a laylay 影片播放-計畫成果簡介

0955-1000 mipapelu' atu musakamu ku tabakiay mikuwanay a tademaw 長官貴賓致詞，計畫主持人致詞

1000-1010 mitanam ku milecuhan a subal 孵育場體驗

1010-1015 misasing 大合照

1015-1030 mukan atu mihanhan 茶敘交流
