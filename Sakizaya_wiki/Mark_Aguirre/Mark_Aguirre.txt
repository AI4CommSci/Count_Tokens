Mark Aguirre(馬克•阿吉雷)
10 bulad 12 demiad, 1959 a mihca, Mark Aguirre, miunduay. 
Mark Aguirre nalecuhan i Chicago, Illinois, nanu syubay kanas-mali miunduay nu Amilika NBA nisakaput, u adidi’ay ayaway mimaliay.
Mark Aguirre i 1981 a mihcan NBA sapapili’ mimaliay a kawaw, saka 1 ku uwac atu silsil 1, namapili’ tu nu Dallas Mavericks putiput, pahanhanan tu kawaw ayza.
