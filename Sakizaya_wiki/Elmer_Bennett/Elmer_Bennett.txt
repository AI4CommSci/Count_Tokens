Elmer Bennett (埃爾默·貝内特，美國NBA聯盟職業籃球運動員)
Elmer Bennett nalecuhan i Evanston, Illinois, nanu syubay kanas-mali miunduay nu Amilika NBA nisakaput, pahanhanan tu kawaw ayza. Elmer Bennett i 1992 a mihcan NBA sapapili’ mimaliay a kawaw, saka 2 ku uwac atu silsil 38, namapili’ tu nu Atlanta Hawks putiput.
2 bulad 13 demiad, 1970 a mihca, Elmer Bennett, miunduay.
