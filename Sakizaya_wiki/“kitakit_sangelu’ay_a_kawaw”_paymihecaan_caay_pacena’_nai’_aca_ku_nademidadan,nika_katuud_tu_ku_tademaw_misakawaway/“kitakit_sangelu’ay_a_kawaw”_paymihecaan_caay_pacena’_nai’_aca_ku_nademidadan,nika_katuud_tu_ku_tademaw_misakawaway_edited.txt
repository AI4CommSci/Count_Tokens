u cananay ku mahiniay cacaya a kawaw ,cacay lipay amisakakawaw cacay a lasubu tulu izaw ku lima a tatukian a misakawaw,tuwaca ngaleb katanaya', mutizeng min tu tatukian,nai' tu ku lawad mipacena', u kasatiman sa, 
cayay pihibang ,nai' ku nademiadan,satu u mahiniay a kawaw, mamin i kitakit a izaw amala ku cacay a bataan mang I'k a tademaw ku misakawaway.....
