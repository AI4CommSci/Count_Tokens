 Jorge Lopes Bom Jesus (若熱·洛佩斯·博姆·熱蘇斯) 

u siwkay niza(簡介)
u pilecuhan ni Jorge Lopes Bom Jesus (若熱·洛佩斯·博姆·熱蘇斯) i Agua Grande (阿瓜格蘭德縣).
nalecuhan ciniza i 7 a bulad 26 a demiad 1962 a mihcaan.
pahezekay niza i tabakiay a cacudadan nu  Sao Tome and Principe (University of São Tomé and Príncipe 聖多美與普林西比大學). mala ku sasaydan (博士) niza.
