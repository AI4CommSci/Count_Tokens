Abdulla Yameen(阿卜杜拉·雅門)[smr’zyut miru’ | Smr’zyut miru’ Yuensma’]

mrhuw Maldives ta’ misuw hya’ ga Abdulla Yameen, aring ryax tay 17 squ byacing tay 11 sa kawas 2013 lga, musa’ spazyang balay ms’rux kya la.

pinqbaqan nya[smr’zyut miru’ | Smr’zyut miru’ Yuensma’]

Abdulla Yameen tmasuq mqwas biru sa Beirut pqwsan biru krahu (American University of Beirut). Abdulla Yameen tmasuq mqwas biru sa Claremont 研究所 (Claremont Graduate University).

Abdulla Yameen hiya’ ga, sswe’ ni Maumoon Abdul Gayoom. Fathimath Ibrahim ga, kneril ni Abdulla Yameen.
