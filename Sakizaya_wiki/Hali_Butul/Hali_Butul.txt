Hali Camu Butul (Amilika a kamu: Harry James Potter; ci 哈利 詹姆 波特 ku hulam a kamu)
u cay katatenga a tademaw a nisanga'an nu nikakilukilul a cudad, u "Hali Butul" a nikakilukilul a cudad nu misulitay ci JK Luling hananay. u cudacudad nu nisatahatahaw ni Hali Butul, mukilim cinida tu nikatinengan i cinidaan sa, u mapalaway ci Hali Butul, maydih malasakapahayay a mapalaway ci nida, u cudad nu nitanetanengan micudad nida i cacudadan u Hu-ke-wa-ce (Amilika a kamu: Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry) sa,
