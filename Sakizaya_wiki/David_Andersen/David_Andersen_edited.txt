David Andersen nalecuhan i Melbourne, Victoria, nanu syubay kanas-mali miunduay nu Amilika NBA nisakaput, pahanhanan tu kawaw ayza. David Andersen i 2002 a mihcan NBA sapapili' mimaliay a kawaw, saka 2 ku uwac atu silsil 37, namapili' tu nu Atlanta Hawks putiput.
1 bulad 23 demiad, 1980 a mihca, David Andersen, miunduay.
