mrhuw Algeria ta’ misuw hya’ ga Abdelaziz Bouteflika, aring ryax tay 27 squ byacing tay 4 sa kawas 1999 lga, musa’ spazyang balay ms’rux kya la.[1]

pinqbaqan nya[smr’zyut miru’ | Smr’zyut miru’ Yuensma’]
