Dai-ni（二集團部落）
Itiniay i Taoyuan(桃園) Cen no Kaohsiung(高雄) ko Dai-ni a niyaro’,  59  ko sa’osi no parod no loma’, 173 ko sa’osi no tamdaw.
96% ko ka’aloman no Yincomin(原住民), polong han i, 166  ko tamdaw; o roma sato i, 4% ko ka’aloman no roma a finacadan, polong han i,   7 ko tamdaw.
O pa-sin-to(百分比) no ka’aloman no tamdaw no kasafinacadan(族群) i, ko Bunun(布農族)90%, Cou(鄒族)2%, Paiwan(排灣族)1%, Tayal(泰雅族)2%, Rukai(魯凱族)1%, roma(其他)1%.
Ira ko picodadan(寶山國小) i niyaro’.
