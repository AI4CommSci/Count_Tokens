u dadiw nu Sakizaya: Sakizayaay a tademaw (族人歌)
misangaay tu dadiw: caykatineng tu (曲: 不詳)
misangaay tu sulit: caykatineng tu (詞: 不詳)
u balucu’ nu misulitay : 
u Sakizaya a tademaw. u tatengaay a tademaw. 
caay katalaw ku cilal atu udad. talabuyu’, 
tala sauwac, tala cuwacuwa limulak ku balucu’, 
kapah ku demiad sananay.

u sakizaya hananay a tademaw.
