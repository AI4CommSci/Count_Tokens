Cal Bowdler nalecuhan i Sharps, Virginia, nanu syubay kanas-mali miunduay nu Amilika NBA nisakaput, pahanhanan tu kawaw ayza. Cal Bowdler i 1999 a mihcan NBA sapapili’ mimaliay a kawaw, saka 1 ku uwac atu silsil 17, namapili’ tu nu Atlanta Hawks putiput.
