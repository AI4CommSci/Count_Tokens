Gjorge Ivanov I 1960 a miheca saka 5 folad saka 2 a romi'ad masofoc ci Gjorge Ivanov, malamikomoday a tamdaw. O Sawara'an no kitakit no Macedonia anini i ci Gjorge Ivanov, patirengan a romi'ad i 2009 a miheca saka 5 folad saka 12 a romi'ad. His second and the last presidential term ended on 12 May 2019.
