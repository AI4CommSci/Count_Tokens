paran（巴蘭部落）
Itiniay i Ren’ai Cen no Nantou ko paran a niyaro’, 686  ko sa’osi no parod no loma’, 1,940 ko sa’osi no tamdaw.
30% ko ka’aloman no Yincomin, polong han i, 590  ko tamdaw; o roma sato i, 70% ko ka’aloman no roma a finacadan, polong han i, 1,350 ko tamdaw.
O pa-sin-to(百分比) no ka’aloman no tamdaw no kasafinacadan i, ko Seediq17%, Tayal10%, Bunun2%, Paiwan1%.
