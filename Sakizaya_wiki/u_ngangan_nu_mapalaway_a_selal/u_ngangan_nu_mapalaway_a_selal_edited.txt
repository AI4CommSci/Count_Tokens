20180630-dabek nu minanam tu kamu nu Sakizaya. u sapinanam a sulit, namakay lalangawan minanam tu Sakizaya a kamu.
paylipay a demaid, minanam ku binacadan nu Sakizaya, tayza i Lou-Cendsinsasaydana luma', ninama tu Sakizaya a kamu. u lawadnu cacay a sapicudadsa, enemay a tuki.

sakatulu a sapicudad sa, u " namakay uzip nu tademaw minanam tu kamu", sakasepat a sapicudad sa, u "namakay kalabian minanam tu kamu", 
sakalima a sapicudad sa, u "namakay dadiw minanam tu kamu", sazikuzan a sapicudad sa, u "namakay lalangawan minanam tu kamu".
