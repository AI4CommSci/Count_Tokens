Russia（俄羅斯）[smmalu patas | ida npatas sspgan ka smmalu patas]

Gaga kska Yacow ka Russia, 60 00 N, 100 00 E ka gaga na nniqan. Kana ka knlbangan na o 17,098,242 sq km（hangan na o Tg1） (knlbanga dxgal o 16,377,742 sq km, knlbangan qsiya o 720,500 sq km). Kana ka sejiqun o niqan 142,355,415 hiyi. Gaga Moscow ka pusu alang paru, jiyax 12 idas 6 ka jiyax skrayan klwaan. Manu ka dxgal Russia ga wada sugan 13.10% ka dxgal qpahan, 49.40% ka dxgal bbuyu, rahuq dxgal do niqan 37.50% na.

Bukung klwaan ta sayang o kiya ka Vladimir Putin, pnaah hngkawas2012 idas 5 jiyax 7pnrajing kmlawa klwaan.
