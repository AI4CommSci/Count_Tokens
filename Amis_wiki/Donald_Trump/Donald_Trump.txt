Donald John Trump(唐納．約翰．川普)

I 1946 a miheca(年) saka 6 folad saka 14 a romi’ad masofoc(出生) ci Donald John Trump, malamikomoday a tamdaw.
O Sawara’an no kitakit(國家) no Puerto rico（波多黎各）, United states（美國） anini i ci Donald John Trump, patirengan a romi’ad i 2017 a miheca saka 1 folad saka 20 a romi’ad. Pimmanaw ni Obama iti opisinana idi Enero 20, 2021.
