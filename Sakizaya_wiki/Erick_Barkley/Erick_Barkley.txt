Erick Barkley (艾瑞克·巴克利,是美國NBA聯盟職業籃球運動員)
Erick Barkley nalecuhan i Queens, New York, nanu syubay kanas-mali miunduay nu Amilika NBA nisakaput, pahanhanan tu kawaw ayza. Erick Barkley i 2000 a mihcan NBA sapapili’ mimaliay a kawaw, saka 1 ku uwac atu silsil 28, namapili’ tu nu Portland Trail Blazers putiput.
2 bulad 21 demiad, 1978 a mihca, Erick Barkley, miunduay.
