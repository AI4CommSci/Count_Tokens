Cory Alexander nalecuhan i Waynesboro, Virginia, nanu syubay kanas-mali miunduay nu Amilika NBA nisakaput, pahanhanan tu kawaw ayza. Donald L. Adamse i 1995 a mihcan NBA sapapili’ mimaliay a kawaw, saka 1 ku uwac atu silsil 29, namapili’ tu nu San Antonio Spurs putiput.
6 bulad 22 demiad, 1973 a mihca, Cory Alexander, miunduay.
