Darrell Arthur, unu syubay kanas-mali miunduay nu Amilika NBA nisakaput(NBA聯盟), u tabakiay ayaway mimaliay. Darrell Arthur i 2008 a mihcaan NBA sapapili’ mimaliay a kawaw, saka 1 ku uwac atu silsil 27, namapili’ tu nu New Orleans Hornets putiput. nalecuhan ciniza i 3 a bulad 25 a demiad, 1988 a mihcaan, Darrell Arthur, u miunduay nu kawaw niza.
