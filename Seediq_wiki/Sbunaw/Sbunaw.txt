= Sbunaw（雪霧鬧部落） =
Gaga mniq Tawyuensu Fusingcyu ka alang Sbunaw.

Kana ka kleegan seejiq ga, Tayal 87%, Amis 1%, Seejiq/Seediq/Seejiq/Seejiq/Sediq 1%, ni rahuq na o 9%.
