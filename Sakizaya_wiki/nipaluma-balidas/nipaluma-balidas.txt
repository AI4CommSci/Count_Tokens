山棕 sancong
山棕（學名：Arenga engleri）為棕櫚科桄榔屬下的一個種。呈棕櫚形，叢生灌木狀，株高可達3公尺，幹莖短。春季開花，肉穗花序，黃色。夏至秋季結果，核果球形或倒卵形，熟果色。羽狀複葉，小葉闊線形，疏齒緣。疏葉椰子類，質感粗。淺根植物。

u sulit nu Amilika
Arenga engleri, or the Formosa palm, Taiwan sugar palm, dwarf sugar palm, or Taiwan arenga palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family. The plant rarely grows more than 10 ft. tall, with a stem diameter of 6in. and a spread of 16 ft. The palm is native to Taiwan as well as Japan's Ryukyu Islands. The fruit of the palm is known to cause a severe allergic reaction.
