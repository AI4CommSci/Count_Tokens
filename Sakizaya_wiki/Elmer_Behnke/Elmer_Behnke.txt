Elmer Behnke (埃爾默·本克，美國NBA聯盟前職業籃球運動員)
Elmer Behnke nalecuh i Rockford, Illinois, nanu syubay kanas-mali miunduay nu Amilika NBA nisakaput. Elmer Behnke i 1951 a mihcaan NBA sapapili’ mimaliay a kawaw, saka 4 ku uwac atu silsil 37, namapili’ tu nu Rochester Royals putiput. nalecuhan ciniza i 2 bulad 3 demiad, 1929 a mihcaan, Elmer Behnke, u miunduay ciniza.
