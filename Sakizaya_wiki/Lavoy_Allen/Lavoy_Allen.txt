Lavoy Allen (Hulam a sulit: 拉沃伊·艾倫) nalecuhan i Trenton, New Jersey. Lavoy Allen unu syubay kanas-mali miunduay nu Amilika NBA nisakaput. Lavoy Allen i 2011 a mihcan NBA sapapili’ mimaliay a kawaw, saka 2 ku uwac atu silsil 50, namapili’ tu nu Philadelphia 76ers putiput. 
2 bulad 4 demiad, 1989 a mihca, Lavoy Allen, miunduay.
