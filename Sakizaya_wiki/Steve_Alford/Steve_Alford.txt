Steve Alford(史蒂夫·埃爾福特)

siwkay niza
11 bulad 23 demiad, 1964 a mihca, Steve Alford, miunduay. 
Steve Alford nalecuhan i Franklin, Indiana, nanu syubay kanas-mali miunduay nu Amilika NBA nisakaput.
Steve Alford i 1987 a mihcan NBA sapapili’ mimaliay a kawaw, saka 2 ku uwac atu silsil 26, namapili’ tu nu Dallas Mavericks putiput.
Steve Alford unu syubay kanas-mali miunduay nu Amilika NBA nisakaput, u misimaw zikuzay mimaliay, u mikilac zikuzay mimaliay.
