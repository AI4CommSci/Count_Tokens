Bob Arnzen nalecuhan i Covington, Kentucky, nanu syubay kanas-mali miunduay nu Amilika NBA nisakaput, pahanhanan tu kawaw ayza. Bob Arnzen i 1969 a mihcan NBA sapapili’ mimaliay a kawaw, saka 8 ku uwac atu silsil 103, namapili’ tu nu San Detroit Pistons putiput.
