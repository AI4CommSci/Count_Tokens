sapibetik a kamu nu pakakawsan
20180630-dabek nu minanam tu kamu nu Sakizaya, u sapinanam a sulit, mudadiw minanam tu Sakizaya a kamu. tina sapicudad sa, ci Tebisapikelid tu minanamay miasip atu misulit. paylipay a demaid, minanam ku binacadan nu Sakizaya, tayza i Lou-Cendsinsasaydana luma', ninama tu Sakizaya a kamu. u lawadnu cacay a sapicudadsa, enemay a tuki. sakacacay a sapicudad sa, u "namakay maplaw minanam tu kamu", sakatusa a sapicudad sa, u "namakay sapibetik minanam tu kamu", sakatulu a sapicudad sa, u " namakay uzip nu tademaw minanam tu kamu", sakasepat a sapicudad sa, u "namakay kalabian minanam tu kamu", sakalima a sapicudad sa, u "namakay dadiw minanam tu kamu", sazikuzan a sapicudad sa, u "namakay lalangawan minanam tu kamu".
