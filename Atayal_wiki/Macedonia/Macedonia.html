Macedonia (馬其頓) hya’ ga cyux maki’ tay 41 50 N, 22 00 E na Oco syuw.

kwara’ kinghciyan niya’ 25,713 sq km (maki’ tay 150ginkgan kin wahci’)

(kinwhci’ niya’ ga 25,433 sq km , kinwhci’ na lawsayan wsilung hya’ ga 280 sq km)
