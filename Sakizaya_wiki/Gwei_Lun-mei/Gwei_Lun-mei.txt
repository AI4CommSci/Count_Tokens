 Gwei Lun-mei(桂綸鎂) 

u siwkay niza
Gwei Lun-mei, u tatayna a palaway nu Taywan. nalecuhan i 12 bulad 25 demiad, 1983 a mihca. namipalaw ku " semilaway a panan" sa, singangan tu i igaay. salungan ciniza, u palaw i makapah tu , sisa maala ku Kingma kawlah i Taywan. u Kingma iga kawlah sa, u sakapahay a iga kawlah i Taywan.

u sulit nu Hulam
桂綸鎂（1983年12月25日－），台灣知名女演員，因電影《藍色大門》開始廣受矚目。

u sulit nu Amilika
Gwei Lun-mei  (born December 25, 1983) is a Taiwanese actress. She started her acting career in 2002, with the film Blue Gate Crossing.
