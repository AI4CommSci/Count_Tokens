Bidhya Devi Bhandari (碧雅·戴維·班達里)


I 1961 a miheca (年) saka 6 folad saka 19 a romi’ad masofoc (出生) ci Bidhya Devi Bhandari, malamikomoday a tamdaw.

O Sawara’an no kitakit (國家) no Nepal (尼泊爾) anini i ci Bidhya Devi Bhandari, patirengan a romi’ad i 2015 a miheca saka 10 folad saka 29 a romi’ad.
