 badukuk 
瓦氏鳳尾蕨  akenek kanen ku badukuk.  mangazuway a badukuk palamelen tu titi misakabi.

siwkay nu babaungen
badukuk sa, u lemangawan kuyni, taneng mukan, akenek. u kapahay a lami'. sabelen ku mangazuay a kaway , asu'. manamuh mukan ku Sakizaya. mauzip ku badukuk i buyubuyuan.
