Dave Bing nalecuhan i Washington, D.C., U.S., nanu syubay kanas-mali miunduay nu Amilika NBA nisakaput, pahanhanan tu kawaw ayza. Dave Bing i 1966 a mihcan NBA sapapili’ mimaliay a kawaw, saka 1 ku uwac atu silsil 2, namapili’ tu nu Detroit Pistons putiput.
