demiadː 2018.03.11 14:30-17:30
kakitizaanː Nabakuwan (Kalingku) a se Sakul niyazu'  Kofuli Saungay nu binawlan(活動) teban a  luma'(中心)
u heci nu matatengilː
1.Walewalelan(荖葉林):
itiza iteban nu Tungao東澳 atu Hanpen漢本. walel sananay ku uway(藤) nu bila'(荖葉). wale-walel-an, yadah ku walel i buyu', sisa pangangan tu Walewalelan.
naayaway a babalaki tayni miadup atu milami', maazih itini yadah ku walel, sisa, panganganen ku Walewalelan.
tina kakitizaan itiza i Yilan a kuwan, ayza sa u Talukuay a subal tuway.
amis 24"30'12.91, wali 121"50'04.35 katukuh amis 24"29'49.32 wali 121"49'43.00, ilaed 640m.
2.Lalabian(夜宿之處)
ipabaw nu Sakul niyazu', i buyubuyuan, i masazukuay(陵線，像山峰或斗笠處) nu buyu'.
sakamusa ku babalaki, natayza miadup atu milami' i buyu', anu caykaletep(來不及) taluma', itiza tu milabin. sisa, panganganen ku Lalabian. 
3.Kamukamulan(油桐樹的地方)  
ipabaw nu buyu' nu 佳山基地Katangka. kamul sananay u kilang 油桐樹, si sangelacay a balu, bangcalay a kilang, u Ngayngayay cidekay a kilang.  
amis 24"02'20.14 wali 121"34'15.85
