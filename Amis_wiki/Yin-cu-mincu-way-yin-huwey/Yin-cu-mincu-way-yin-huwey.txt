Yin-cu-mincu-way-yin-huwey （原住民族委員會）
Yin-cu-mincu-weyin-huwey (sapuru han a panganga –Yin-min-huwey)
u miebaway, u mipadamaay tu demak nu masaniyaniyaru’ay a tarakaway a Seyhu nu mita i Taywan.
Na i 1996 a miheca tu sabaw tusa a bulad a nipatireng.
U miebaway, u padamaay tu masama:anay a demakan nu yin-cu-min i Sey-hu.
