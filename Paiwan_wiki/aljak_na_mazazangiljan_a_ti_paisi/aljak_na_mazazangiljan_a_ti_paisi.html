minamilingan aicu "aljak na mazazangiljan a ti paisi (白雪公主)", mavan a milimilingan nua sedjerman (German) i iyurupu (Europe) tua tja kakedriyan. a i sangasangasan a vinecikan aicu a milimilingan i tua sinansunatj nua madrusa a kaka a lja grimm (The Brothers Grimm) a minamilingan nua lja grimm (Grimms' Fairy Tales) ka 1812 a caviljan. sinanljavaran aicu a "Sneewittchen" nua i kakaian a djermany.
