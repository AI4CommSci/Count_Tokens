batad nu wali (kamu nu Hulam: [1])
u katinengan nu salengacay a tademawi "tipay".
u batad nu wali hananay ku sawalian nu "nutipan nu Europe".  
i tepal ku "untipan nu Europe", u "tepalay a wali"hananay. izaw ku "nuwalian nu O-Cuo" (East Europe, ), "nuwalitimulan nu  Europe", "nuamisan nu Africa" atu Turkey. u "inutebanan nu walian"hananay izaw ku "nuwalian nu Africa", Li-Fan-Te (, Lavent[2]), A-La-Po pangkiw a subalatu "inutebanan nu Asia".   

"batad nu wali" saan sa, namaka kakitidaan atu kitakit nu European a tademaw, u lala' i batad nu wali mueneng ku takuliyaway a tademaw. sakawalian sabataday a China, Korea North (, ), atu Japan (, ), u "nuwalian nu Asia"hananay. sisa u batad nu wali saan sa. izaw ku henay Siberia, u "nuamisan nu Asia"hananay, atu "walitimulan nu Asia".
