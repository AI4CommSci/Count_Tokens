Pnyahan Wikipidiya

Kesun daha knklaan hengak (Phonetics, hengak: /fəˈnɛtɪks/) we, knklaan kenkyu hengak na kari. Egu riyung ka knklaan quri kari, knklaan hengak ka kingal. Dungus na bale we, skadi pusu bale na hengak na krkari, so ka "Huwa ta kesun phrenang ka hengak", ini quri so ooda bale ye dngdungus na hengak. Icil na we, knklaan gaya hengak, skadi ma smalu gaya hnghengak na kingal kari.

Skdiyun na knklaan hengak ka hengak, kesun daha hengak pnhrnagan seediq, kii si hengak na kari.

Patis hengak kkklangan (IPA) snalu Pssliyan Slluhe Knklaan Hengak Kkklangan nii we, ngalun daha pnstiyu hengak.

Knklaan Swaye[smmalu patas | ida npatas sspgan ka smmalu patas]

Teru ka knklaan swaye na knklaan hengak:

  • Knklaan phrenang (articulatory phonetics)
  • Knklaan hrenang (acoustic phonetics)
  • Knklaan qnbhangan (auditory phonetics)

Rekisi[smmalu patas | ida npatas sspgan ka smmalu patas]

Ado ka mksmalu kingal patis pnstiyu hengak ka seediq, tkriyak ka knklaan hengak hndure. Betaq 19 Sencuri de, tkriyak ka phiti hrenang di. So kiya de, leexan ta hmiti hrenang ma hengak, pkrana knklaan hengak.
