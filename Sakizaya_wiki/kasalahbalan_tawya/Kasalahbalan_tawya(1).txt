cacay a lasubu a mihcaan siwaay a bulad tusa a bataan idaw ku sepat a demiad
namulekal i sananal, tayda i kakalanaman malanam, mamin tu malanam, namusulimet tu luma’, yadah ku tatuni’ i kalahukan, mamin tu musulimet, miidid tu titi kami atu laluma’an mapulung, idaw ku heci nu katalalan, basis atu buting, micakay tu yadahay a lami’.
awda hantu laluma’an atu cabay tayni mukan tu titi, mabecul tu.
