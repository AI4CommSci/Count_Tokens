u siwkay niza(簡介)
u pilecuhan ni António Costa (安東尼奧·科斯塔) i Râmnicu Vâlcea (勒姆尼庫沃爾恰).
nalecuhan ciniza i 4 a bulad 1 a demiad 1972 a mihcaan.
pahezekay niza i tabakiay a cacudadan nu Iowa (Grinnell College Iowa State University 愛荷華州立大學格林內爾學院). maala ku kasedan (碩士) niza.
