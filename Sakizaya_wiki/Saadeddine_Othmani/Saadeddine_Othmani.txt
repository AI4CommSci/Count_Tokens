 Saadeddine Othmani 

u siwkay niza(簡介)
u pilecuhan ni Saadeddine Othmani i Inezgane nu Morocco (摩洛哥伊奈茲甘).
nalecuhan ciniza i 1 a bulad 16 a demiad 1956 a mihcaan.
namaala ku misaydangay a sasaydan (MD Ph.D 醫學博士) .

micakat (就職)
u cunli (總理 Prime Minister) nu Morocco (摩洛哥) ayza sa ci Saadeddine Othmani,
micakat a demiad sa i 2017 a mihcaan 3 bulad 17 demiad.
